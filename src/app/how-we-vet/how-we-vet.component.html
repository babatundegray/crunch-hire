Our Vetting Process

Our rigorous freelancer screening process ensures we get the best. As Project consultants ourselves, we look for proven experts with passion and drive.

We only accept people into the network who we’d want to work with and learn from ourselves. Hundreds of Project consultants apply to CrunchHire each month. We accept less than 5%.

STEP ONE

Recruit Top Performers

We monitor Facebook groups, forums, and LinkedIn, and proactively place job descriptions and ask for referrals to find the very best freelance project team. Top freelancers who learn about CrunchHire can also apply for admission.

STEP TWO

In-Depth Skill Review

We review each project consultants professional experience, client feedback, and work samples to assess their level of expertise and knowledge in applying project management “best practices” in practical situations. Most freelancers also complete a skill-specific assessment.

STEP THREE

Video Interview

Our final layer of screening is a video interview that assesses project management expertise, communication skills, problem-solving skills, project management tools and client relationship management skillset. If all above criteria are met, the freelancer joins our CrunchHire community.

STEP FOUR

Test Projects

After acceptance to CrunchHire, each candidate is assigned a test project to evaluate whether they can “walk the walk.” Test projects provide real-world scenarios for candidates to demonstrate their competence, thoroughness, professionalism, and integrity over what is typically 1-3 weeks.

STEP FIVE

Continued Excellence

CrunchHire expect candidate to maintain a perfect track record while working with clients. We check-in with clients every 2 weeks to ensure quality service and do not tolerate sub-par work or poor communication for any reason.
