Frequently
Asked Questions

  • How to Download Mevron Passenger App

  • When you want to enjoy the safety and security of the Mevron platform, you need to download our Passenger app. It’s free for download to your mobile device. You simply need to determine your operating system (and be certain they meet the minimum requirements, listed below):
  • For iOS (compatible with iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch, 10.0 iOS or later)
  • For Android (9.0 or later)
  • For Windows phones (Windows 8.0, 8.1, and 10.0 only, but only cash payments are permitted with Window currently)

  • How to Use Mevron App on a Smartphone

  • It is possible to use the Mevron app and order a ride from any smart device, so long as your device is connected to an active Internet connection and has GPS activated.
  • Log in to your Mevron app account. You can also order a ride through our Mobile Site, but this will only allow for cash payment options.

  • How to Request a Ride

  • It’s simple to request a ride through the Mevron app.
  • From the app:
  • Search your destination
  • If you need to add more stops, tap the (+) icon. To remove a stop, tap the (x) beside it.
  • When finished, choose Done
  • Select the category according to your preferences
  • Choose your Pickup location
  • Tap Confirm Order
  • You have the option to add up to three stops to any trip.
  • Once the trip has officially started, you cannot add more stops, but it is possible to adjust your destination.
  • Whenever you place multiple stops in your trip, that’s the order the Driver will make, even if they don’t equal the shortest distance or duration.

  • Canceling a Trip

  • It is possible to cancel a scheduled trip, but only before the ride officially starts. In some situations, though, a Ride Cancellation fee may still be assessed, even if it is canceled before the trip commences.
  • To cancel the ride, from your Mevron app:
  • Select Cancel on the home screen
  • You will be asked to give a legitimate reason for the cancellation. Feedback is important to us. Repeated cancellations may lead to a suspension of Mevron services for a specified length of time.
